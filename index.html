About Me

I am an Associate Researcher in European Law at the Jean Monnet Centre of Excellence (UFMG) and an Associate Professor at the School of Accounts of the State Court of Auditors of Minas Gerais (TCE-MG)[cite: 3]. My expertise lies at the intersection of International Public Law and Public Policies, with a core focus on climate change governance, sustainable investments, and energy transition[cite: 3].

Research

My current research frameworks investigate multi-level governance, green taxonomies, and climate justice mechanisms[cite: 3].

Doctoral Thesis: "Regulation and mixed investments in the European Union taxonomy: a path towards a just energy transition." (UFMG / Université Paris Cité)[cite: 3].
