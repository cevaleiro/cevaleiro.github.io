Intro

I am an associate researcher and fellow at the Jean Monnet Center of Excellence of the Federal University of Minas Gerais (CEJM/UFMG)[cite: 1]. My academic work bridges Public Policies and International Public Law, focusing on regulatory frameworks for a transition toward sustainability[cite: 1].

Currently, I am conducting an international research stay as a Visiting Researcher at Leuphana Universität Lüneburg in Germany[cite: 1].

Research

My doctoral thesis and core projects analyze multi-level climate governance, green taxonomies, and climate justice mechanisms[cite: 1]. Key research interests include:

  • European Union Green Policy Framework & Sustainable Taxonomies[cite: 1]
  • Regulation of Just Energy Transitions & Mixed Investments[cite: 1]
  • Regional Integration Law & Refugee Protection Policies[cite: 1]
